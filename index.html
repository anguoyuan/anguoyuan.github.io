Research topic

My research focuses on large-scale, high-accuracy visual retrieval, emulating the brain's ability to retrieve perceptions for various tasks. I work on two main fronts: firstly, I automate the indexing and recognition process by finding consistencies based on common sense or world models, enhancing efficiency; secondly, I'm redefining the ideal entities for general vision retrieval systems, expanding beyond conventional image or video units, to accommodate diverse tasks. Together, these efforts contribute to advancing the field of visual retrieval with potential applications in large-scale, high-precision contexts.
